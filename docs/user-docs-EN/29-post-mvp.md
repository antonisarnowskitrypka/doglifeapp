# What's Next (the roadmap)

The platform launches with a complete set of core features, but we already have a list of things we want to add later. These are **plans for the future** — they're not in the app yet, but we know they're coming.

## New ideas

- **Mobile app (iOS and Android)** — a native app to install from the store, not just the browser version.
- **Pay by phone (Tap to Pay)** — the provider accepts a card payment directly on the phone, without a separate terminal.
- **Weekly tasks from the trainer** — the trainer sets up recurring weekly tasks for you and your pet (an extension of the current homework reminders).
- **Achievements** — badges and milestones for you and your pet (e.g., completed courses, streaks) shown on the pet's profile.
- **Pet travel passport** — a section in Life of Pet that gathers trips, vaccinations relevant to travel, and entry requirements.

## On the roadmap (things put off for later)

- **Premium subscription for the owner** — a paid plan that unlocks extra features (mostly health-related).
- **Health reminders** — automatic reminders about vaccinations and deworming at set intervals.
- **Veterinary integrations** — a connection with health facilities.
- **Full-text search** — searching by any words from descriptions, with typo tolerance.
- **Travel surcharge calculated by kilometers** — currently travel has a flat-rate price.
- **Full fiscal invoices** — for the launch we issue simplified invoices.
- **More premium features and higher plans for providers** — the Pro subscription (€11/mo) launches already in the MVP with Boost and automatic Google Meet links; further add-ons and plans later.
- **Referral cashback** — a share of the commission from referred providers.
- **Custom certificate templates** — designing your own certificate look instead of a ready-made background.
- **Ratings of individual staff** — separate averages for individual team members.
- **Pet travel and activity rankings** — playful leaderboards tied to the travel passport.

> The list isn't closed — if you have an idea, write to us via [Contact Support](./22-contact-support.md).
