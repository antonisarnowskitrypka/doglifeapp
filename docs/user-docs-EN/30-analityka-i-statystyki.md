# Analytics & Insights

The analytics panel shows how your business is doing on the platform — from search impressions to bookings and reviews. Some of the data is **free**, while advanced analyses and forecasts are unlocked by the **Pro** subscription (see [Business Dashboard](./19-provider-dashboard.md)).

> **No AI.** The forecasts don't use any "artificial intelligence" — they're purely statistical calculations from **your own history** (averages, trends, seasonality). Everything is reproducible and can be explained with a number.

## What you get for free

- **Impressions** in search results and **clicks** (plus **CTR** — how many people clicked out of those you were shown to).
- **Bookings and revenue over time**.
- **Cancellation rate** and **no-show** rate.
- **Repeat clients** — what share comes back.
- **Top services** and **most-booked hours** (top 3).
- **Average response time** to messages.
- **Average rating** (weighted) and number of reviews.
- **Price comparison — a hint**: whether a given service is "below / around / above" the market median (without specific numbers).

For free you see history going back up to **30 days**.

## What Pro adds

- **Conversion funnel**: impression → click → booking → completed service, with drop-off at each stage (also broken down by position in results / mode / category).
- **Demand map (7×24)** and **"high demand, no slots" hints** — we show the hours when people are looking for you, but you have no availability then.
- **Forecasts** (no AI): e.g., expected bookings next week, end-of-period revenue forecast, **clients at risk of leaving** (based on how long ago and how often they booked).
- **Full price comparison** — the median and the range (25th–75th percentile) for your category and area.
- **Trust score** — your private quality rating (see below).
- A list of clients to win back, a per-employee breakdown, and a **longer history — up to 90 days**.

## How to read the forecasts

Every forecast has a **confidence level** that depends on the sample size:

- **low** (little data) — treat it as a rough guide,
- **medium**,
- **high** — plenty of data, greater reliability.

It's always an estimate "based on your history", not a guarantee.

## Price comparison privacy

Comparisons are **anonymous and aggregated**:

- we show only **medians and ranges**, never the prices of a specific competitor,
- a benchmark only appears once there are **enough providers** in the group (a minimum threshold), so that no one can be "guessed",
- we compare only within **the same currency**, category, area and level.

## Trust score

This is a **private** value calculated from signals you already have: average rating, share of verified reviews, share of completed services, reliability (few cancellations/no-shows) and response speed. New and small businesses are treated fairly (the score "pulls" toward the platform average until you accumulate data).

In this version, the trust score is visible **only to you** — it's not shown publicly and **does not affect** your position in search. It's a hint about what to improve.
