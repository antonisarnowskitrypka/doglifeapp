# Banning a Client

A provider can ban a client who does something unacceptable.

## Scope

- The ban is **per provider (organization)** — it blocks the client only with that provider, not across the whole platform.
- It applies to the **client (owner)** and covers all of their pets.

## Effect

When a client is banned:

- **New bookings are blocked** — the client can no longer book any of this provider's services.
- **Future confirmed bookings are cancelled** with a **full refund**, under the same rules as a [provider cancellation](./10-booking-lifecycle.md) (no penalty for the client).
- Earlier/completed bookings and their history remain untouched.

## Notes

- The provider can record the reason for the ban.
- The ban can later be lifted by the provider.
- Platform-level bans (abuse across multiple providers) are a super-admin matter, separate from this provider-level ban.
