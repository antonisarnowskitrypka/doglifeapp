# Reviews

Clients can leave a review for a provider after a completed service.

## Who can leave a review

- Only the **client** of a completed booking (reviews are one-sided: client → provider).
- One review per completed booking.
- If the client **manually confirms** the appointment ("Confirm session") instead of waiting 24h — they can leave a review **right away**. Otherwise the review request arrives 24h after the service (see [Notifications](./16-notifications.md)).

## What a review contains

1. **Star rating** — one overall rating from 1 to 5.
2. **Highlight tags** — quick positive tags in the Bolt style. The client picks any that fit, e.g.:
   - "Great specialist"
   - "Good location"
   - "Friendly"
   - "Punctual"
   - "Clean"
3. **Text** *(optional)* — in the client's own words (public, on the profile).
4. **Feedback for the provider** *(optional)* — a separate field visible **only to the provider**, never on their profile.

## Loyalty review

After using **X services** with the same provider (default 3, set by the platform), the client **one-time** unlocks an extra review with **greater weight** — it counts more heavily toward the provider's average rating and toward search ranking.

## Company rating and specialist rating

Every review counts **twice**:

- toward the **company average** (shown on the provider's profile),
- toward the **personal average of the specialist** who performed the service.

The specialist's rating is attached to **their account**, so it **travels with them** — if they change companies or work at several, their reputation stays with them. On the specialist you can see reviews from all the companies they have worked at; those earned elsewhere are **labeled** ("at another provider"), so the score adds up but the source is always clear.

In search we rank **by company rating** — the specialist's personal rating does not change it.

### Guest host

If an event had a [guest host](./11-packages.md), you'll get **two separate review requests**: one for the event/company and one for the guest themselves. The guest's rating goes **only toward their personal reputation** — it does not affect the host company's average.

## Verified reviews

Reviews for services **booked and paid through the platform** carry a **"Verified"** badge — proof that the reviewer actually used and paid for the service. Reviews linked to manual bookings (off-platform) do not get this badge.

## Where reviews appear

On the provider's profile, in the **ABOUT** tab (see [Provider Profile](./06-provider-profile.md)):

- Average rating (weighted — loyalty reviews count more heavily)
- Counts of the most frequently awarded highlight tags
- Individual reviews with text and the Verified badge where applicable

The "feedback for the provider" field **never** appears on the profile — it goes only to the provider.
