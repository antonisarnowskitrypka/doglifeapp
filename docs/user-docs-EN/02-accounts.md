# Creating an Account

How a person creates and sets up their account.

## Sign-up methods

- E-mail + password
- Google
- Apple
- Facebook

## Phone number — required and verified

A phone number is **mandatory** and **verified by SMS (one-time code)** during sign-up. This prevents people from creating multiple accounts to game referral rewards (see [Referrals](./20-referrals.md)).

## Profile

Every person can set:

- A profile **photo / avatar**
- A short **bio**

Pets can have avatars too — see [Pets](./04-pets.md).

## Company details for invoicing

A user can add **company details** (name, tax ID, address) to their account and request that the invoice for a service be issued to the company rather than to a private individual — see [Provider Terms & GDPR](./08-terms-and-gdpr.md) and the invoicing section in the [Provider Business Dashboard](./19-provider-dashboard.md).

## One account, many roles

The same account can take on different roles — no separate logins:

- **Starting a business** — anyone can open their own provider business through the provider wizard (a solo trainer is also an organization; see [Provider Setup](./17-provider-setup.md)).
- **Being hired as staff** — you can also be invited to work as a trainer in someone else's organization.

A user can be the **owner** of their own organization and at the same time a **staff member** in another one.

## Being invited as staff

When an organization adds you in the **STAFF** tab:

1. The owner enters your e-mail address.
2. You receive an invitation.
3. You accept it — your account is linked to that organization as a staff member.

If you don't have an account yet, the invitation stays **pending** until you sign up with that e-mail, after which you can accept it.
