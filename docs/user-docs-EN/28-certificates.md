# Certificates

For **events** and **courses (packages)**, the platform generates PDF certificates.

## When they're created

Automatically upon completion:

- **Event** — after it ends, for enrolled participants.
- **Course (package)** — after all sessions have been used.

The client gets a notification, and the certificate (PDF) lands in their documents.

## What's on the certificate

The certificate is built on a **background predefined by the platform**, with predefined placeholders for:

- **the owner and the pet** (e.g. "Jan Kowalski with dog Lanza"),
- the course / event name,
- the school (organization) name,
- the logo,
- the date,
- the provider's **signature**.

In the settings, the provider uploads their **signature as a PNG** and a logo.

## Post-MVP

Custom certificate templates.
