# Platform Overview

A marketplace + SaaS platform that connects pet owners with professional service providers.

## What it's for

Pet owners can find, book and pay for services for their pets. Service providers manage their calendar, clients, sessions and payments all in one place.

## MVP scope

1. Service booking and marketplace
2. Online payments
3. Provider and staff calendars
4. Session workspace (notes, homework, attachments)
5. Pet history timeline
6. Group events
7. Training packages

## After MVP

- Pet owner subscription
- Pet travel and activity leaderboards
- Veterinary integrations
- Health features in the subscription
