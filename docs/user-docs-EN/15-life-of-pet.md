# Life of Pet

A SaaS feature that lets owners keep a complete digital record of their pet's life.

## Choosing a pet

An owner can have several pets (see [Pets](./04-pets.md)). In this tab the owner chooses which pet they are currently viewing — documents, health data, and the timeline are separate for each pet.

## Documents

- Passport details
- Microchip number
- General notes

## Health data

- Vaccination history and upcoming vaccination dates
- Deworming schedule
- A place for notes from vet visits and test results
- Automatic reminders (see [Notifications](./16-notifications.md))

## Activity timeline

A coherent, chronological view of everything the pet has been doing. Two sources:

- **On-platform activity** (automatic) — training sessions, physiotherapy visits, group events, etc., generated from completed bookings.
- **Events added by the owner** (manual) — e.g. a visit to another specialist, test results, notes from the vet. Each can have attachments.

A manual event can be added to **several pets at once** (e.g. a shared vet visit), keeping separate results and attachments for each pet — see [Pets](./04-pets.md).

### Sharing with providers

The owner can share selected timeline events with a specific provider, giving them fuller context before a session. Sharing is **per provider**, **revocable**, and managed from the booking panel — see [Booking Detail & History](./13-booking-detail-and-history.md).

> The event timeline and its sharing are part of the MVP. Health reminders and premium subscription features will come later (see below).

## Premium features (post-MVP)

An optional subscription unlocking extra features for pet owners — the exact scope is to be determined.
