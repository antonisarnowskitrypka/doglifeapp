# Provider Terms & GDPR

Every provider can publish their own **terms** and **GDPR / privacy policy**, which the client accepts when booking.

## Provider setup

The provider can add and edit:

- Their **terms**
- Their **GDPR / privacy policy**

## Client acceptance

- The client accepts the provider's terms and policy when booking a service.
- The acceptance is remembered **per provider** — the client isn't asked on every booking.
- If the provider **publishes a new version**, the client will be asked to accept again on their next booking.

## Record

Each acceptance is saved together with the document version, so the provider has proof of what the client agreed to and when.
