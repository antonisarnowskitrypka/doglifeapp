# Referrals

Growth rewards for inviting new users. (Discount codes and timed promotions run by the provider are described in the [Business Dashboard](./19-provider-dashboard.md).)

## Client referrals

When a new **client** signs up through someone's referral:

- **Both** sides — the new client and the referrer — receive a **one-time 5% discount**.
- The discount is **funded by the platform** — it doesn't reduce the provider's earnings.
- Each reward is **one-time** per person, to be used on a booking.

## Provider referrals

When a new **provider** signs up through another provider's referral:

- **Both sides** — the new and the referring provider — get **−1 pp commission** for **an extra month**.
- **Months count, not percentage points.** Each additional referral adds **+1 month** of discount; the discount itself is always **−1 pp** (three referrals = 3 months at −1 pp, not −3 pp).
- The discount applies to the plan's base rate (with subscription → 3.5%, without → 6.5%) and is **paused** when the platform grants a special rate (the accumulated months aren't wasted).
- The size of the discount (1 pp) is configurable by the platform.

## Post-MVP

- **Cashback** for the referring provider — a share of the platform's earnings from the referred provider's transactions, for a certain period after onboarding.
